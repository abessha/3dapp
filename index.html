Cola



Coca-Cola is one of the world's most iconic beverage brands,

known for its signature cola soft drink. Launched in 1886, the brand has become synonymous with refreshment and happiness, featuring a distinct red and white logo that is recognized globally. Coca-Cola is celebrated for its classic, crisp taste and has expanded its product line to include a variety of beverages, but it remains most famous for its original cola formula. The brand is also known for its memorable advertising campaigns and strong association with moments of joy and togetherness.

Coca Cola

Coca-Cola is a globally recognized soft drink with a distinctive cola flavor, first introduced in 1886. The drink is made from a blend of carbonated water, high fructose corn syrup or sucrose, caramel color, phosphoric acid, caffeine, and natural flavorings.

Sprite

Sprite is a crisp, lemon-lime flavored soft drink that is caffeine-free and known for its refreshing taste. Introduced by The Coca-Cola Company in 1961, it features a clear, effervescent formula that blends lemon and lime flavors with carbonated water and sweeteners.

Fanta

Fanta is a vibrant, fruit-flavored soft drink that originated in Germany during World War II. It is known for its bright colors and a wide range of fruity flavors, such as orange.
